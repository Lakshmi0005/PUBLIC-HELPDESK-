Travel and Tourism in India

Overview

India is one of the most diverse and culturally rich travel destinations in the world. From the Himalayas in the north to the serene backwaters of Kerala in the south, and from historical monuments to spiritual journeys, the country offers a wide spectrum of experiences.

Why Travel India?

  • Explore UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Rich spiritual and cultural traditions
  • Wildlife and eco-tourism experiences
  • Adventure tourism and trekking
  • Ayurveda, wellness, and yoga tourism

Ministry of Tourism

The Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, is responsible for the development and promotion of tourism in the country. It formulates national policies and programs and coordinates with central and state agencies to promote tourism both domestically and internationally.

Initiatives & Campaigns

Some notable initiatives include the globally acclaimed Incredible India campaign, Dekho Apna Desh webinars, and the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD schemes for infrastructure development.

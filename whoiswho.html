President of India

The President of India is the ceremonial head of state and the supreme commander of the Indian Armed Forces. The position is currently held by Droupadi Murmu, who is the first tribal woman to hold this prestigious office.

Learn More

Vice President of India

The Vice President serves as the Chairman of the Rajya Sabha and acts as President in case of vacancy. The current Vice President is Jagdeep Dhankhar.

Learn More

Prime Minister of India

The Prime Minister is the head of the government and the leader of the Union Cabinet. Narendra Modi is currently serving as the Prime Minister of India.

Supreme Court of India

The Supreme Court is the highest judicial forum and final court of appeal. It consists of the Chief Justice of India and 33 other judges appointed by the President of India.
